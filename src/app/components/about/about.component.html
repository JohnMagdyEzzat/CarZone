About Our Car Booking System

A smarter, simpler way to book and manage cars online.

✨ What We Offer

  • ✉️ Easy Booking: Reserve cars with just a 10% deposit.
  • 🚗 Real-Time Availability: See which cars are currently available.
  • 🧤 Flexible Refund Policy: Get a full or partial refund based on cancellation time.
  • 🏋️ Maintenance Tracking: Smart alerts for car part servicing.
  • 💳 Secure Payments: Powered by Stripe for seamless transactions.
  • 👤 Personalized Experience: View your booking history and manage your profile.

🚀 Built with Modern Tech

Our project uses Laravel for a powerful backend and Angular for a dynamic, responsive frontend. We focus on security, speed, and simplicity for the best user experience.
